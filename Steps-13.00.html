Grouping the Delivery Years by Decade 13



1.First, we will go back to the report view and right click on Year, which should be under the Calendar Table.



2. Once the dropdown appears you will click New Group and a Groups window will appear 
3. This window is where we will be able to group the years by decade so that the visual is easier to view than having over 30 years of data individually shown by each specific year. Within this window we will change to Group Type to List instead of Bins.
4. Now that the years are listed in front of us we are now able to group them. In order to group them you will have to click each year that you want grouped while holding the CTRL key on the keyboard. 
5. We will be grouping the years into decades, for the first group select year 1955 – 1959, these will be the 1950s.
6. Once selected press the Group button towards the bottom.
7. Once selected, a group will appear on the right-hand side box under Groups and Members. Double click the new group and rename it to 1950s
8. Repeat this process for all the decades, there should be 8 decades in total
