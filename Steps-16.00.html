Inserting Logos 16

Colaberry Logo and Boeing logo are in the ZIP Folder provided in the beginning
  1. Click the Insert tab from the ribbon
  2. Select image option and browse for the “Boeing Logo White” in your computer
  3. Select “Boeing Logo White” Logo
  4. Once loaded onto the dashboard click on the image and head to the Format Pane
    1. General:
      1. Properties
        1. Height: 96
        2. Width: 336
      2. Position 
        1. Horizontal: 24
        2. Vertical: 0
    2. Click the image:
      1. Format: Bring to Front
